The Dream SMP

The Dream SMP (formerly known as the Dream Team SMP), is a private, whitelisted survival multiplayer (also known as SMP) Minecraft server played on by the Dream Team and their friends. It is famous for being a roleplay themed server with a mostly improvisational plot and a long history of alliances, wars, factions, eras, and characters.
